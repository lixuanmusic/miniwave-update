# Miniwave Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** November 2025

## Overview

Miniwave ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our audio player application.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Anonymous Identifiers
- **User ID**: We generate a local, anonymous UUID to distinguish between different user sessions. This ID is not linked to your personal identity.
- **Storage**: This ID is stored only on your device and is not shared with third parties.

### 2. App Usage Data
We collect the following app usage data to improve our product and user experience:

- **App Launch Information**: App version, build number, platform type, user type (free/trial/paid), color theme preference
- **Audio File Operations**: Audio file duration (in minutes), whether the file contains existing markers, file opening method (double-click/drag-and-drop)
- **Playback Behavior**: Playback start, playback position seeking (seek source), waveform zoom operations, volume adjustments
- **Marker Operations**: Marker creation, deletion, saving to file, copying to clipboard, marker panel opening
- **Subscription-Related**: Upgrade button exposure and clicks, subscription dialog display, subscription actions (trial/purchase)

### 3. Device Information
- **Platform Type**: macOS
- **App Version Information**: Version number and build number

## Information We Do NOT Collect

We **do NOT** collect the following information:
- ❌ Personal identification information (name, email address, phone number, etc.)
- ❌ Location information
- ❌ Device unique identifiers (such as device serial numbers, MAC addresses, etc.)
- ❌ Audio file content
- ❌ Specific marker text content
- ❌ File paths or file names you open
- ❌ Any information that can identify you personally

## Purpose of Data Collection

We collect data solely for the following purposes:

1. **Product Improvement**: Analyze how users interact with the app, identify usage patterns, and optimize user experience
2. **Feature Development**: Understand which features are most popular and guide product development
3. **Issue Diagnosis**: Identify and fix problems in the app
4. **Subscription Analytics**: Analyze subscription conversion funnels and optimize subscription experience

## Data Storage and Transmission

- **Data Service Provider**: We use PostHog (https://posthog.com) as our analytics service provider
- **Data Storage Location**: Data is stored on PostHog's servers (which may be located in the United States or European Union, depending on your selection)
- **Data Transmission**: Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS)
- **Data Retention**: Data retention follows PostHog's terms of service

## Data Sharing

We **do NOT**:
- Sell your data to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use your data for advertising targeting

We **only**:
- Share anonymous usage data with PostHog (for analytics services)
- Disclose data when required by law

## Your Rights

### 1. Access and Control
- You can disable analytics through app settings (if this option is available)
- You can disable all data collection by modifying the app configuration

### 2. Data Deletion
- Since we use anonymous identifiers, you can reset your user ID by deleting and reinstalling the app
- To request deletion of collected data, please contact us (see contact information below)

### 3. Opt-Out of Data Collection
- You can completely disable data collection by modifying the app configuration `AnalyticsConfig.enableAnalytics = false`

## Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
